​Privacy Policy

​1. Intro

We care about your privacy and want to keep your info safe. This policy tells you how we collect, use, and protect your info when you use our forum, blog, or platform.

2. Info We Grab

From Users:

Your name, email, username, and password

Profile info, your chosen avatar, and what you like

Your posts, comments, replies, likes, and saved stuff

What you subscribe to (threads, authors, or topics)

Any messages or reports you send to the team

From Everyone:

Your IP address, browser type, device info, and operating system

Pages you went to, time spent here, and what you clicked

Cookies and analytics data

Optional Stuff:

Contact forms, surveys, and feedback

3. How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

Make and manage your account

Show your posts, comments, and replies

Send updates, replies, or notifications about stuff you follow

Keep track of who is following which authors or topics

Manage content and keep things friendly

See how the platform is doing and make it better

Answer questions or deal with problems

Stop spam, abuse, and bad activity

4. Who Sees Your Info

We might share data with:

Team members: To check content and keep the rules

Analytics tools: To see how people use the platform

Hosting services: To store data

The Law: If the law makes us

We don't sell your info to anyone.

5. Cookies

We might use cookies to:

Keep you logged in

Remember stuff you like and what you subscribe to

Measure how many people engage for analytics

Suggest stuff you might want to see

You can control cookies in your browser's settings.

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your posts, comments, and account info as long as your account is open or it's needed for the platform. You can close your account or ask us to delete your data.

7. What You Can Do

You can:

See and download your posts and comments

Change or delete your profile info

Ask us to close your account

Change your email notifications and tracking

8. Keeping Your Info Safe

We use security to protect your info. But be careful when sharing private stuff in public posts or comments.

9. Kids' Privacy

This platform is not for kids under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from them.

10. Policy Updates

We might change this policy sometimes. Check back here for the latest version.
